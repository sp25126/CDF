# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Teachers and students in Haryana government schools. Teachers use it as a co-pilot to explain concepts and run quizzes; students view it on smartboards/projectors.

## Product Purpose
Shiksha Sahayak (AI Teaching Assistant) - An MVP for Haryana Government Schools to simplify complex concepts and facilitate interactive classroom learning through voice and visuals.

## Brand Personality
Teacher-friendly, student-focused, encouraging, innovative, and calm. It should feel like an expert but accessible companion in the classroom.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, clinical or overly technical interfaces, overstimulating "AI slop" (random gradients, side-stripe borders, decorative animations), and low-contrast UI.

## Design Principles
- **One primary action per area**: Reduce cognitive load by focusing on one thing at a time.
- **Large touch targets**: Optimized for tablets and smartboards.
- **High readability**: Large font sizes and high contrast for classroom visibility.
- **Low clutter**: Remove unnecessary UI elements; focus on teaching materials.
- **Clear state feedback**: Ensure the teacher always knows what JULI-E is doing (idle, listening, thinking, speaking).
- **Motion with meaning**: Use animation only for state changes and focus, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast for body text).
- Large, legible font scales for distance viewing.
- Support for reduced-motion settings.
- Touch-friendly targets (minimum 44x44px).
